The Most Important Thing in Life

© Paulo Bittencourt

Why do we live?

What’s the purpose of living?

What do we live for?

To eat?

Drink?

Work?

Have a big house and a big car?

Have sex?

What is life’s goal?

Life has many goals?

Which, then, is life’s ultimate goal?

There must be a goal that is more valuable than all other goals.

The all goals’ goal.

Think about it.

Think.

Harder!

What do we want the most?

I think what we want the most is to be loved.

Can someone seriously claim being loved is not the most important thing in life?

Seriously.

The most important.

Even a book that contains horrible stuff (the Bible) says that the greatest of all things is love.

That without love we are nothing.

Nothing!

What’s love?

“Love is patient and kind, never jealous, boastful, proud or rude. Love isn’t selfish or quick tempered. It doesn’t keep a record of wrongs that others do. Love rejoices in the truth, but not in evil. Love is always supportive, loyal, hopeful and trusting. Love never fails!”1 Corinthians 13:4-8

I don’t need to believe in an invisible man in the sky, to agree that that is love.

If that is love, most people don’t love correctly.

The way they should.

Myself included.

How I fall short of loving like that!

We want to be happy.

Happy!

Can anyone be happy without being loved?

I don’t think so.

If yes, that’s an exception.

To be happy, the overwhelming majority of people need to be loved.

Need.

No love, no happiness.

For love to exist, it must be produced.

We need to love.

Love to be loved.
